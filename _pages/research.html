My current research projects:

  • Sampling vs. optimization: Exploit tools from optimization and sampling to obtain convergence guarantees for optimization algorithms

  • Distributed optimization: In a setting where data is distributed across devices or servers we are interested in how to define the information exchange to balance communication costs and convergence speed

  • Federated learning and privacy: Understand how to guarantee privacy in learning algorithms

  • Explainability: Relating the output of a deep neural network to simpler-to-analyze objects in order to gain insight into their behavior

  • Data generation: Generating datasets to enhance small datasets for the purpose of trainng models or to create datasets which are realistic but account for privacy constraints
